The Fashionable Harpsichord

Who keyed my quasi-piano?

At first glance I knew Schroder would love it. It's a fine machine and a beautiful piece of art.

But would he be able to play such an instrument? What with his modern jazz stylings and his friend Pig Pen always lurking around, a foe of a bath and bar of soap if I had ever seen one. Even still, with his head lowered and his shoulders hunched, he would take no pause and play Mozart with alacrity and skill.
